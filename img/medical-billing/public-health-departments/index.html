Low Cost - Easy to Implement Medicare Claims Processing and Department of Health Billing Systems

TransactRx offers the most cost effective complete healthcare billing solution for Health Departments to bill for Pediatric vaccines, Adult vaccines and healthcare services. With one easy to use web based healthcare billing software application you can bill Medicare Part B, Medicare Part D, Medicaid, Medicaid VFC and commercial payers for any vaccine or healthcare service.

TransactRx is the only solution available today that in one application can bill both Medicare Part D pharmacy benefit claims and standard professional medical claims. TransactRx built in all of the coding and billing rules so you don't have to hire specialized billing staff to submit accurate and correctly coded claims. One step claims processing includes patient eligibility verification and fee schedule pricing of the claim.

Turn-Key Department of Health Billing Software

TransactRx provides a complete solution for your Health Department to bill and manage accounts receivables for all of your pediatric and adult services.

  • Easy to use web based application - no software or systems to install
  • Unlimited training is provided via webinar
  • Supports billing for all pediatric and adult vaccines and medical services
  • Submit claims to Medicare Part B, Medicare Part D, Medicaid, Medicaid VFC, Healthy Families and commercial payers
  • Check patient's benefit eligibility and financial responsibility
  • Medicare claim fee schedule based product and administration reimbursement amounts returned in real time
  • Reporting to manage outstanding and paid claims
  • Low one-time setup fee and no monthly fees. Low per claim transaction based pricing
